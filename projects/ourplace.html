OURPLACE

Ourplace is a safe and easy to use mobile application designed to help users find their ideal rooms or roommate(s) based on their needs and unique preferences.

Problem:

The surge in rent prices are causing young adults to seek roommates to split the cost of rent but the task of finding a compatible, reliable, and trustworthy roommate on and offline has become stressful, tedious, and difficult.

Solution:

Successfully help individuals find and connect with compatible and reliable roommates.

    Duration

  • 3 weeks

    Team

  • 4 people

    Role

  • Project Manager
  • UX Researcher
  • UI Designer

Hypothesis Statement:

We believe people are having a hard time finding their most compatible roommate, how might we create an app that matches renters with like minded people also searching for a place to live; how might we use “OURPLACE” to eliminate the stress of finding reliable roommates in world class cities.

Interview:

our team followed qualitative research and design thinking methodologies to gain insights into users’ experiences searching for compatible roommates. We contacted 6 interviewees along age 18-30 people living in NYC borough to gather more useful insights about their challenges and motivations.

Competitor Analysis

What analyzed other applications relating to roommate finding to have a better understanding strategies and their market goals. We examined competitors' features, differentiators, strengths, weaknesses, and customer reviews by analyzing those competitors so we could improve our products and services.

User Persona

We targeted users and audiences on roommate finder based on the assumptions of stakeholders. It allows us to begin designing and building immediately without getting overly bogged down with the details of user behavior. We created a profile to understand their needs and challenges better the persona helped us which better solutions due to a deeper understanding of users' pain points, needs, goals, and general characteristics.

Affinity Diagram

We grabbed our interview feedback and grouped them into individual sections. We organized many ideas into their natural relationships from a brainstorming session. Use it to generate, manage, and consolidate information related to a product, process, complex issue, or problem.

Empathy Map

We use this method to gain a deeper insight into our customers. What they might say, think, does, and feels to learn about their behaviors and decisions. It externalizes knowledge about users in order to create a shared understanding of users' needs and aid in decision-making.

Story Board

We devised a user story that vividly visualized how the user might face during each phase of the story. This step is designed to help our clients feel how our user might feel, making it easier for us to share and explain our vision with visualizations.

User Insight Statement

A recent art school graduate needs to feel less stressed about starting her career while managing the cost of living in a big city, without the help of a roommate, life can be overwhelming mentally and financially.

Point of View Statement

“OURPLACE” was designed to help individuals successfuly find roommate. We have observed that other similar app like “Roomi” isn’t providing a smooth process, poor customer service and development, which is causing user anxiety and abandoning. How might we improve “APP NAME” so that our roommate seekers are successful based on an increase in the number of new contracts signed, customer referrals, positive reviews, and a lower bounce rate.
