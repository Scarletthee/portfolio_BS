OURPLACE

Ourplace is a safe and easy to use mobile application designed to help users find their ideal rooms or roommate(s) based on their needs and unique preferences.

Problem:

The surge in rent prices are causing young adults to seek roommates to split the cost of rent but the task of finding a compatible, reliable, and trustworthy roommate on and offline has become stressful, tedious, and difficult.

Solution:

Successfully help individuals find and connect with compatible and reliable roommates.
